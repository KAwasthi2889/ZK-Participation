Smart Contract: ClinicalTrialCredential

416-line Compact contract. 8 circuits. Compiled and verified.

Circuits

  • issueCredential — Sponsor-only authorization
  • revokeCredential — Instant on-chain revocation
  • generateProof — Patient witness verification
  • verifyProof — Public verification
  • hasActiveCredential — Status query
  • isRevoked — Revocation check
  • getCredentialHash — Commitment lookup
  • getCredentialCount — Issuance metrics

Privacy Model

Patient identityNEVER on-chain
Trial detailsNEVER on-chain
Completion statusNEVER on-chain
Issue dateNEVER on-chain
Proof result (valid/invalid)Public

Live Demo: 2 Minutes

01

Sponsor: Connect & Issue

Sign in as sponsor@cipher.com. Connect wallet. Issue credential to patient address mn1_7f3a... for trial NCT-2024-0041. Credential stored in confidential state.

02

Patient: Generate Proof

Sign in as patient@cipher.com. Connect wallet. Click "Generate Proof" — the smart contract re-computes the commitment hash from the patient's private witness. Returns a ZK proof JSON.

03

Verifier: Submit & Validate

Sign in as verifier@cipher.com. Paste the proof. Click "Verify". Contract checks: credential exists? commitment matches? not revoked? Result: ✅ Valid
