# Product & AI Prototyper (Person 4)

## Your Mission
Your goal is to accelerate the rest of the team and own the final presentation.

### 1. Design & UX Polish
- Take the basic frontend components built by Person 1 and use AI to generate polished, premium styling.
- Ensure the user flow (Sponsor -> Patient -> Verifier) is intuitive, responsive, and looks like a winning hackathon project.

### 2. Pitch Deck & Storytelling
- The tech alone doesn't win hackathons—the story does.
- Create the pitch deck explaining the problem (privacy in clinical trials) and our Midnight/ZK solution.
- Write the demo script so the presentation flows perfectly within the time limit.

### 3. QA & Testing
- Be the dedicated tester. Try to break the application from an end-user perspective.
- When you find a bug, use AI to diagnose the issue and hand a potential fix over to the integration or frontend developers.

- **Phase 2: Core Development (Hours 5 - 15)**: Generate mock data so the frontend can be built without waiting for the smart contract. Act as a QA tester for early UI components.
- **Phase 3: Integration (Hours 15 - 22)**: Polish the UI alongside Person 1. Draft the demo script and finalize the pitch deck slides.
- **Phase 4: Polish & Deployment (Hours 22 - 27)**: Relentlessly test the end-to-end deployed flow. Tweak copy, descriptions, and tooltips in the app.
- **Phase 5: Demo Prep & Buffer (Hours 27 - 30)**: Lead the demo rehearsal. Direct the team to ensure the demo hits the time limit perfectly during practice runs.
